## Getting started

At Serpa, we prioritize an app-first mentality. Whether its the front end, the backend, or the database, our focus is on delivering a seamless and intuitive user experience. In this documentation, we will guide you through the process of getting started with our tool, providing you with the necessary information to maximize its potential and create exceptional applications.

> An app is an entity that abstracts the git repository, the type of application, and the way it is compiled. From an app, different deployments can be generated in different environments.
