## Getting started

At Serpa, we prioritize an app-first mentality. Whether it's the front end, the backend, or the database, our focus is on delivering a seamless and intuitive user experience. In this documentation, we will guide you through the process of getting started with our tool, providing you with the necessary information to maximize its potential and create exceptional applications.

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card
    title="Apps"
    icon="Apps"

  >
We believe in harnessing the power of containers to unlock endless possibilities for application development. With our stack, you can leverage the full potential of containerization and Kubernetes to build robust and feature-rich applications. Containers provide a lightweight and isolated environment, ensuring seamless deployment and scalability.
   </Card>
  <Card
    title="Compilations"

    Our tool automates the application development process, ensuring fast build speed and eliminating the need for manual environment provisioning. With seamless compilation capabilities, you can quickly transform your code into a deployable format, optimizing performance for efficient deployment.

  </Card>
    <Card
    title="Deployments"
    icon="rocket"

  We allow you to have complete end-to-end control over the deployment process, allowing you to manage every aspect of your application's release. You can easily choose which version of your application goes live, perform rollbacks if needed, and even pause deployments when necessary. Our platform provides a seamless and intuitive interface for code-to-URL publishing, giving you the flexibility to decide whether your application should have public or private access.
